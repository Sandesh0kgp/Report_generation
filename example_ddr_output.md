# Detailed Diagnostic Report (DDR)

## Executive Summary
The property displays medium-to-severe concern particularly focused on localized moisture intrusion in Bedroom 1 and the Kitchen area. It is highly recommended to address the plumbing leak immediately to prevent further structural degradation. Overall, structural integrity appears stable, but water damage risks require prompt mitigation.

## 3. Probable Root Cause
The dampness in the Kitchen is driven by an active plumbing leak under the sink cabinet (trap failure). The minor scaling in Bedroom 1 was likely caused by past external seepage through exterior cracks during heavy rain, which have since been sealed, as no active thermal signs were found.

## 4. Recommended Actions
- [High] Plumber inspection and trap replacement under the kitchen sink.
- [Medium] Monitor Bedroom 1 east wall during upcoming rainy seasons.
- [Low] Re-grout tile joints in the Master Bathroom shower enclosure.
